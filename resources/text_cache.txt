Emva kwale ncoko uVusikhaya waxhumela inkabi yehashe kayise,
wayixhuzula kwakanye, yabizwa yasabela, yeva endleleni.
Yaya kusithela entlanjeni ngasediphini, amanqina egabadula,
eshiya kusiphuka izicithi. Yagabadula njalo, yaya kusithela kumlambo iThina, ayabuye ibonwe muntu.
UMaQhudeni naye zange aphozi se maseko akumka uVusikhaya.
Wanxiba impahla yakhe yesiNtu ngokungxama okukhulu
okwakhona kkhatswa zizibilini eziphezulu. Entloko walungisa isiqhova
sakhe esasakhiwe ngeeqhiya ezintathu. Wasijija kakuhle saza
saveza uphondo olunye ecaleni. Akugqiba wangxala uzalipholile
wakhe ngecuba lesiNtu. Eliseleyo walinyhala enxilini yakhe.
Kaloku intombi le yakwaQhudeni yayiyile mbo ingazange iwubone
umnyango wesikolo. Yayikwiqela elalibala yimitshotsho xa
imingqandendana yeentanga zakhe yayihamba imigama emide, isiya
kwezinye iilali ezazisele zakhelwe izikolo.
Kungekabi lisesha elingakanani emkile uVusikhaya, wabekeka
endleleni uMaQhudeni, enyathela ezinyathelweni zakhe. Wahamba
ebathaza ngezikaTshiwo loo mgama mde ukusuka emzini wakhe
eNtabayikhonjwa ukuya kowabo eNgojini. Kwezi lali kwakubethwa
ngezikaTshiwo nanjengoko zazingekabikho izithuthi ngenxa
yokungabikho semgangathweni kweendlela zeenqwelo. Abantu
babepila kukusebenzisa amahashe okanye babethe ngezikaTshiwo.
Isithuthi sasibonwa kanye ngeveki, nakhona xa kuthe kanti
sesabalimi okanye unovenkile othile oya okanye osuka edolophini
ukuya kuthenga impahla yevenkile.
Wadlula iilali ngeelali ezinamagama uMaQhudeni enyathelela
phezulu. Wanqumla phakathi kwamasimi, ehamba ngemilimandlela.
Entloko wayethe ngcu ixakatho lakhe, ngesandla sasekhohlo
ejingisa ibhekilana yamarhewu. Wayelungiselela ukuthoba unxano
kwindawo anokuphumla kuyo.
